How do you embed a video or audio in your page?

To embed a video in your page, you may use the <video> tag with the "width" and "height" attribute. To specify which video to embed, nest a <source> tag in the <video> and use the "src" attribute and the video's path as its value. You must also indicate the type of the video or its format such as MP4 or other video supported type.

To embed an audio in your page, you may use the <audio> tag similar with video, specifying which audio to embed requires a nested source tag with the "src" attribute and the audio's file path or location as its value.

What are forms in HTML?

In HTML, forms allow developers to request an input from their users. Understanding how forms work is essential in developing web applications since it could be on fo the primary sources of data in your application.

How do you create forms in HTML?

To create forms in HTML, you may use the <form> tag with the "action" attribute which specifies the location or file which processes the data from the user.
